Use this when

Use this page to inspect shared primitives and feedback components such as modals, dialogs, toasts, select, and toggles.

Do not use this when

Do not keep adding substantial workflow demos here by default. When a helper becomes large or task-specific, give it a dedicated page instead of overloading the general utilities playground.
